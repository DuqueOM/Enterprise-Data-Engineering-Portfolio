This file lists concrete steps to achieve the requested goals for P4.

1) Ablation study (chunk size, top-k, embedding models)
- Use: python scripts/ablation.py --chunk-sizes 600,1000,1500 \
        --models sentence-transformers/all-MiniLM-L6-v2,all-mpnet-base-v2 \
        --topk 1,5 --smoke
- Report saved to results/ablation_report.csv. For full runs, remove --smoke.
- Inputs controlled by:
  * P4/src/ingestion/scraper.py --chunk-size
  * P4/src/search/index_knowledge_base.py --model
  * P4/scripts/eval.py --top_k
